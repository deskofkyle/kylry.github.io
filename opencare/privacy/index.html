Privacy Policy for OpenCare This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the OpenCare app. 1. Information We Collect When you use OpenCare, we may collect the following information: Analytics: We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous data about how you use the app. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our services. No personal information is linked to these analytics. Photo Storage: Photos are stored on Google Firebase. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your photos, but please consider your own privacy needs when uploading content. Payments: We process our payments through RevenueCat. OpenCare does not directly handle or store your credit card information. Email List: If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is managed through Mailchimp. We use your email to communicate with you about updates and promotional offers. 2. Use of Information The information we collect helps us to: Improve and optimize OpenCare. Communicate with you, including about new features and updates. Detect and prevent fraud or other harmful activities. 3. Sharing Your Information We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the OpenCare service (e.g., storing photos with Google Firebase or processing payments with RevenueCat). 4. Your Rights You can unsubscribe from our email list at any time by following the unsubscribe link in our emails. You may also request to view, update, or delete any personal information we have about you. 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. 6. Contact Us For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at opencareapp@gmail.com.
